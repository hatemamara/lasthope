Deamon Slayer

Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba (鬼滅の刃, Kimetsu no Yaiba,
"Blade of Demon Destruction"[4]) is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by
Koyoharu Gotouge. It follows teenage Tanjiro Kamado,
who strives to become a demon slayer after his family was slaughtered and his younger
sister Nezuko turned into a demon.
